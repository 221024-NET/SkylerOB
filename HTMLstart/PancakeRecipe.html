Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp soft butter

Step 1

In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking power, salt, and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and butter

Step 2

Heat a lightly oiled girddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour the batter onto the girddle using around 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
